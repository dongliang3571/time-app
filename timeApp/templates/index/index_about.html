About us



Time App has two main components to it. The first component is the admin interface which allows your managers to create employee profiles, generate their Qr ID cards and track their attendance and earnings. The second component is the mobile app. Using an iOS device; preferably an Ipad. Your employees can use the device at you facility to scan their Qr codes in order for them to sign in and out. We make the process effortless.
